About Us

Traditional Craft, Modern Precision

Blending time-honored metalworking techniques with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results.

Botswana's Trusted Metalworks

Located in the heart of Gabane, Tshipi Works has built its reputation on delivering exceptional metal fabrication services for over a decade. Our master craftsmen combine traditional welding techniques passed down through generations with modern precision equipment.

We specialize in bespoke metalwork for residential and commercial clients, ensuring every piece we create meets the highest standards of quality and durability. From initial design consultation to final installation, our team oversees every detail.
